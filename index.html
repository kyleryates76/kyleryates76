What is Computer Renewal?

Computer Renewal is just your local computer repair shop. We take great pride in our work. The world is becoming more tech-savvy as we speak, we are here to acknowledge the technology left behind. Be it any old computer or phone, we take pride in repairing them back to factory conditions. We also deal with today's generation of technology. We can build you a computer, repair your computer, remove viruses and malware, and sell some general computer supplies while we're at it.

Our Goal!

The goal of Computer Renewal is to turn your computer back to how it was when you first go it. Our employees are very experienced in computer hardware and know what it takes to restore life to some of these older computer systems. Stop on by Computer Renewal to quite literally bring your computer back to life.
